About This Project

The Legend

This website was created to tell the famous fable of The Lion and the Tiny Mouse. This story has been told for hundreds of years to teach us that no one is too small to be a big help!

The Author

Retold and designed as a special school project. The colors and drawings were chosen to feel like a real storybook found in the African Serengeti.
