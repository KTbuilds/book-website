The Lion and the Tiny Mouse

The Mercy

Under the scorching sun of the Serengeti, a great Lion lay sleeping. A tiny Mouse accidentally ran across the Lion's paw, waking him. The Lion caught the Mouse and prepared to eat him, but the Mouse pleaded, "Spare me, and one day I may help you." The Lion laughed at the idea of a tiny mouse helping a king, but he let him go.

The Snare

Weeks later, the Lion was caught in a hunter’s rope net. He roared in frustration, unable to break free. The tiny Mouse heard the roar and ran to the spot. Using his sharp teeth, the Mouse gnawed through the thick ropes until the Lion was free.

The Lesson

The Lion looked at the tiny Mouse with new respect. "You were right," the Lion said. "A small friend can be a great friend." From that day on, the King of the Jungle and the tiny Mouse were inseparable, proving that kindness is never wasted.
